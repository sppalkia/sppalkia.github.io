Overview

DAWN is a Stanford University industrial affiliates program and is subject to university policies for such programs including openness in research, publication and broad sharing of results, and faculty freedom to pursue research topics and methodology of their choice. See https://industrialaffiliates.stanford.edu/. DAWN researchers will use and develop open-source software, and it is the intention of all DAWN researchers that any software released will be released under an open source model, such as BSD.
